Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you enquire.

How far ahead should I order?

We recommend enquiring as early as possible, especially for weekends, custom designs, and peak celebration dates.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Yes. Pickup is available, and local delivery is offered across Brisbane depending on suburb and distance.

Can I customise colours and messages?

Yes. Many cakes can be personalised with colour choices, short messages, and simple design preferences.

Do you take corporate or larger event orders?

Yes. We welcome office celebrations, branded cupcake boxes, client gifting, and larger event-style orders.

How is pricing decided?

Pricing depends on cake size, style, level of detail, and any custom additions such as toppers or illustration work.
